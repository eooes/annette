We are the creators and producers of a quality Gelato brand called Annette I tim tuk tuk, selling Homemade Gelato of several tastes and flavors through a unique medium, that is the traditional Thai 3 wheeler, more commonly known as a “Tuk-Tuk”. It is a specially designed mobile Gelato parlor that we take to the customer. Our services can be provided in different parts of the city, at different times of the day/week. One of our main ambitions is to provide sweet-lovers everywhere, the delicious and authentic taste of Gelato.
